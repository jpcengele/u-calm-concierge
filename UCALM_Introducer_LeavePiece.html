Consider it done.

U-CALM is a small concierge house, founded in Lugano in 2013, holding a limited number of families across Switzerland, Italy and the United Kingdom. We work entirely by introduction. We do not advertise. This page exists so that the right introducer — perhaps you — knows what we are, and when to mention us to a family who would value the relationship.

What the house holds.

The work
Aviation, ground, household, protection — coordinated by one named specialist who knows the file. One statement at month-end. Four working languages held in-house: English, Italian, French, German.
The aviation line
U-Calm Aviation — bespoke charter, on-demand, group lift, destination management, executive protection. Vetted operator panel (ARGUS Platinum / Wyvern Wingman / IS-BAO Stage 2 equivalent). Four-hour notice window for enrolled members.
The geography
Lugano is the desk's base. The same English-speaking specialists who hold a London arrangement hold a Milan one. The file does not change shape across borders.
The relationship
Multi-decade by intention. Members come through introduction and tend to stay across generations. The desk is structured around a small number of files held well, not many files held quickly.

The moment

The largest peacetime wealth migration on record is reshaping Milan and Lugano.

WHEN TO MENTION US

The kind of moment U-CALM fits.

A family is closing on a Milan residence and asking who handles the day-to-day. A London family has just relocated to Lugano and is feeling the fragmentation. A new private client has aviation, household, school logistics and a calendar that doesn't sit comfortably in any one supplier's hands. A family-office principal is being asked, again, "who do you use for these things?"

"It might be worth mentioning U-CALM. They sit between the cities and they hold everything on one file."

The right kind of introduction is short. The conversation that follows is the work.

How to introduce.

A single email, copying J-P, with a sentence or two of context about the family. We follow up directly, quietly, at the family's pace. We come to Milan or wherever the conversation needs to happen.

"I've been working with U-CALM in Lugano for years. They run a small house, four languages, and they hold aviation and ground on one file. Worth a quiet conversation when you have the time."

That is enough. The first meeting is a coffee, not a pitch. The relationship — if it is one — begins there.
